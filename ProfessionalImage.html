My Experience

After my freshman year, I had the opportunity to intern as a data scientist at a startup company in the Woodlands, TX called ThoughtTrace. ThoughTrace uses natural language processing (NLP) to analyze oil and gas leases and provides insights accordingly.

During my internship, I participated in SCRUM and completed multiple projects on a need-by-need basis. (1) I centralized and optimized the data labeling system by creating a web application which held all the labeling additions, allowing ThoughtTrace to outsource the labeling process. (2) I validated model enhancements by comparing the results from the old and new model in order to provide QA feedback. (3) I validated the UI by creating a web application to provide the current ontology of classifications for the front-end developers to utilize. And (4) I created a dictionary to increase model accuracy by decreasing the input cardinality, increasing the OCR accuracy by more than 10%. These projects helped the company roll out model 2.0 of “ThoughtTrace” more efficiently.
